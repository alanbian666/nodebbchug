Our Mission

The ocean is the lungs of our planet, providing most of the oxygen we breathe. Yet, it is under siege from pollution, overfishing, and climate change.

Aqua Horizon leverages cutting-edge subsea technology to monitor coral health, clean plastic from gyres, and restore endangered marine habitats. We believe in a future where humanity and the ocean thrive in harmony.
